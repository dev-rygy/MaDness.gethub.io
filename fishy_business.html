Fishy Business

Summary

Fishy Business was a group project created for a game developement class using the Gamemaker engine. The main focus of the project was to simulate an environment and process similar to working with a professional studio. We had deadlines, checkups, peer evaluations, design documentation, and presentations. I was the group leader and the main gameplay programer. A snapshot of this project can be found on our itch.io page.

Gameplay

Fishy Business is a lively fishing game in which the player can freely fish in a small lake environment, sell their catches, and buy new gear. The player starts in a place called the "Overworld" where they can traverse a small lake area. Here, the player can interact with the lake to fish and Fisher Frog to buy and sell gear. The player can buy and upgrade their rod to increase the occurance chance of fish (the chance that a more rare fish will spawn), thier line to increase their tension meter (a meter that appears on the screen when the player is fighting a fish ), and their bait to raise bite chance (the chance that a fish will bite onto their rod). Each fish is unique and come with their own chances and value. The more rare the catch the more valuable the fish is to sell.
