Hell's Kitchen

Summary

Hells Kitchen is a game I made with a small team of programmers for the contest Ludum Dare #50. Ludum Dare is a contest where you are given 72 hours to create a small game based on a particular theme. The theme choosen for this jam was "Every 10 Seconds" so we decided to create a cooking game where Every 10 seconds a new customer comes in. To put a spin on genre we decided to make the game Hell Themed where the player must serve demons in order to survive.

Gameplay

As the player, you are given 5 stars at the beginning and you will lose stars by not serving customers in the time given. There are six stools which can can each house a demon, once the demon approaches their stool an order will appear as a ticket on the left. The game is endless and you must try to survive as long as you can and try to get a high score.
