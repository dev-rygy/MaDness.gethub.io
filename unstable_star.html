Rogue Nebula

Summary

Unstable Star was a game made in collaboration with the Video Game Design Club at Arizona State University. This game features three unique levels and two challenging boss fights. Embark on a lighthearted and action-packed journey of redemption. A former Border Guard for the Intragalactic Coalition, he quickly becomes embroiled in an unexpected Rebellion to save the galaxy. I was the vice president and the systems lead for the project. I overseed project management including, organizing and schedualing tasks for my team and mapping the project timeline.

Gameplay

The gameplay for the game features 3 levels each with unique challenges. These challenges along with two different bosses add varience to the levels. Top down combat is crutial to the game's identity as a bullet hell. Swappable weapons and abilities are offered to the player to encourage gameplay diversity. A dynamic dialogue system helps to convey character and life into the narritive.

Implemented Features

Name Description
Objective System Objectives for each level were made to give varience to each level. These objectives include kill all enemies, survive for a set amount of time, and kill enemies in a set amount of time. The levels follow a linear progression based on this system.
Wave System Dynamic wave system that chooses from a pool of waves unique to each level.
Player Abilities The player is given a small set of unique abilites. A Phase Shift ability gives the player invunerability frames for a set amount of time and a Pulse Bomb explodes and does damage over a large area. Each ability has a cooldown timer that communicates with the player's HUD to let the player know when they can use it again. They also have a use counter that limits the amount of times it can be used.
Player Weapon Arsenal The player has a variety of weapons they can use throughout the game. Each weapon can be unlocked as they complete levels. A pistol that has a slow fire rate, a laser weapon that can charge for higher amounts of damage and go through entities, and a gatling weapon that fires very fast and has a cooldown for balance.
Boss Fights Game features two challenging boss fights. For the first boss the player needs to shoot shield generators to destroy a large bubble sheild. This boss can summon minions to aid in the battle. The second boss has a flury of attacks including a burst atttack, that send out a burst of bullets in a fan, and a sweep attack where the boss goes back and forth continuously firing bullets rapidly.
Dialogue System Full and complete dialogue system with portrates of characters. Text is animated with a typewriter effect. Over 8 cutscenes with dialogue and dialogue plays during gameplay.
Particle System and Shaders The game features unique hit and destroy particles heavily inspired by arcade games. The explosion particles not only use high quality explosions from Unity's particle pack but also voxels to elicite a 3D arcade-like effect. The hit effects reflect the type of entity damaged and change color using a combination of lightning particles and 2D sprites. The player is given a special shader fresnel effect when hit.
Music and Sound Music and Sound made by very talented sound members from the club.
