Tiny Knight

Tiny Kight -- Press Start to Play -- The princess must be saved! Nothing chiche here.

Tiny Kight is a very small game I'm making for experience with the Unity engine and Top Down RPGs. Currently it's the game I'm working on most of the time but much of the code and mechanics I plan to transfer to Spirit of Calamity. Some of the features I have included right now are: Player movement and combat, Enemy movement and Combat, Doors and Keys, etc.
